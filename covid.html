How to stay safe from Covid-19

no need for panic,just be cautious

With Covid-19 spreading across the globe,you must be hearing a lot of recommendations to wash your hands,keep away from public gatherings,etc.

COVID-19,the disease caused by the novel coronavirus,appears to spread primarily when people cough or sneeze and respiratory droplets land on those nearby.
But most experts say that taking some simple precautions may indeed help you prevent the spread of the disease.
Here are some tips that may help you avoid meeting this virus

*Practice good hand hygiene and minimize how often you touch your face

*avoid touching surfaces with your fingertips

*Grab a tissue

*stay home if you can

*Use a face mask if you are feeling sick

*just because you suddenly cough or sneeze, it doesnt mean you must have the virus. Dont panic and get yourself medical attention.
